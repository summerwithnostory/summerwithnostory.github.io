About the contents of this example site

January 5, 2020

All content on this example site is taken from The Birds of Australia, volume 2 of 7, by John Gould.

That book's available in full on Project Gutenbird Gutenberg. The illustrations are beautiful.

Note that many of the names of the birds have since changed, and there's efforts underway in parts of Australia to start identifying these birds by the names used by local Aboriginal Australians.
